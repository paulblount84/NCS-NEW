About NCS

Northridge Community School is a place where students grow in faith, character, and academic excellence together. We believe every student deserves a nurturing environment that challenges their mind, shapes their heart, and prepares them for a life of purpose.

G

Godly Character

Daily devotions, biblical teaching, and mentorship help students grow in faith, integrity, and kindness.

A

Great Academics

A rigorous, well-rounded curriculum from kindergarten through high school taught by dedicated educators.

C

Strong Community

Families, teachers, and students work together to create a supportive home away from home.

F

Family Focused

We partner closely with parents through open communication, family events, and a welcoming community where every family feels known and valued.

Admissions

  1. Submit an Inquiry

    Fill out our online inquiry form and our admissions team will reach out within two business days.

  2. Tour the Campus

    Visit Northridge with your family to meet teachers, see classrooms, and experience our community firsthand.

  3. Complete Your Application

    Submit transcripts, references, and required forms through our secure online portal.

  4. Enrollment Decision

    Families are notified of admission decisions and next steps for enrollment within two weeks.
